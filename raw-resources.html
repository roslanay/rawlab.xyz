Raw Resources
installation, found objects Walking by the Black Sea coast we stumbled upon what at first appeared to be a pile of rocks. However, a closer look revealed that these were remnants of construction materials that only mimic organic forms. As these man-made structures erode and corrode, their constituent elements embark on a transformative journey, shedding their artificial shapes and blending back into the natural landscape. They surrender to the forces of nature, echoing the raw materials from which they once emerged. This cyclical journey, where the boundaries between artificial and natural begin to blur, challenges our notions of permanence and decay in our built surroundings.

presented as part of Sofia Art Week Campo Verità BG 2024
