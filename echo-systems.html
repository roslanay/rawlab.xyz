Echo Systems interactive installation, sound art Echo Systems is an interactive sound installation that uses electronic waste, sensors, and modulators to capture and translate the otherwise imperceptible electromagnetic fre- quencies into sound.

The work responds in real time to its own frequency environment, revealing the hidden sonic landscape shaped by human activity. It functions as a responsive ecosystem, prompting reflection on the geophysical dimensions and materiality of media infrastructures, as well as the invisible impact of technology on our surroundings.
