@rawlab.xyz rawlab is a research-driven artistic duo interrogating the shifting dynamics between language, thought, and emergent technologies. Operating as an open framework for experimentation and play, their practice prompts reflection on the broader socio-cultural implications of our networked environments, transforming audiences from passive observers into active participants and turning interaction into a space for critical engagement.
